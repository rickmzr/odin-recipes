Shrimp Ceviche

Description

This lightning-quick dish for one can easily be scaled up for a group of any size. And it also makes a great appetizer with tortilla chips or crackers. Serves one.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup cooked shrimp, chopped
  • 1/2 small tomato, finely chopped
  • 1/2 small kirby cucumber, finely chopped
  • 2 Tbsp uncooked red onion, chopped
  • 2 Tbsp fresh lime juice
  • 2 tsp cilantro, chopped
  • 1 pinch salt, or to taste
  • 1 pinch black pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine the shrimp, tomato, cucumber, onions, and lime juice; let stand for 10 minutes for the flavors to blend.
  2. Sprinkle with the cilantro; season with salt and black pepper.
